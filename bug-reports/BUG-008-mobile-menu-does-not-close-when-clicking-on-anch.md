## BUG-008 — Mobile menu does not close when clicking on anchor link (same-page navigation)

**Title:** Mobile menu does not close when clicking on anchor link (same-page navigation)
**Environment:** iPhone 14 Safari, Samsung Galaxy S23 Chrome, Astra 4.6
**Severity:** Major | **Priority:** High

**Steps to Reproduce:**
1. Open site on mobile
2. Open hamburger menu
3. Click a menu item that links to an anchor on the same page (e.g., #contact)

**Expected Result:** Menu closes; page scrolls to anchor section
**Actual Result:** Page scrolls to anchor but menu stays open — blocks the content the user just navigated to
